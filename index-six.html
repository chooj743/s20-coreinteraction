I planned to get up early in the morning,

brush my teeth, boil water,

maybe get a double portion breakfast, code,

eat ice cream, walk aimlessly around nearby streets,

escape from coding by grabbing lunch, go to the library,

read a book, do my laundry,

watch a movie, take a nap after,

have a chat over the phone with my friends,

snack on junk foods, update my creative process journal,

head down to a museum, take photographs of anything interesting,

play a new game on phone,

go grocery shopping, prepare the ingredients,

eat dinner, do the dishes,

take out the trash, take a shower,

blow dry my hair, vacuum the floor,

do research on assignments, apply for internships

but I ended up just

planning my itinerary for weekends.
